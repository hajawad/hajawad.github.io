Monopoly Credit Card Terminal

June 2016 - Aug 2016

Developed an iOS application in Swift using Xcode that mocks up the credit card machine in Monopoly Revolution. The application enables the banker in monopoly to register players, add money to a player, transfer money from one player to another, and keep track of all transactions using a real-time database.
